A strong message from the deputy head teacher

I am Mwanje Alex Andrew, I serve as the area Councilor representing Kayabwe Town Council at Mpigi District Local Council, I also serve as the Deputy Speaker Mpigi District. I got the honor of serving as the Deputy Head Teacher at Holy Trinity SSS Nkozi as well. I thank God for all the responsibility he has entrusted with me.

I take this opportunity to welcome you to our Website. I am glad you have taken keen interest in finding out and Knowing more about HOLY TRINITY SSS NKOZI (HOTS), There's no doubt you won't go wrong finding more about us. Holy Trinity is very much aware of Information and Technology as a Catalyst to Success, she is also aware that the world has become a Global Village.

HOTS is an education institution that is dedicated to providing a holistic education i.e. producing Academically sound, God fearing, Socially Balanced, Disciplined and Physically Fit Citizens. This is attained by putting emphasis on Academics, Spirituality, Discipline and Co-Curricular activities. I must say I am proud to be associated with HOTS a school that is driven by the Vision "To be the leading school in the provision of quality education in Mpigi and the country at large". We do our best to realize our dream and not just offer our vision Lip-service. We do whatever it takes to walk the talk. The school has a Conducive environment that facilitates the Teaching-Learning process.

My dear Visitor(s) once again allow me express my heartfelt gratitude to you for taking off some time to find out and know more about HOLY TRINITY SSS NKOZI.Surely your Knowledge about ... us adds color to who we are. May God bless you.
MWANJE ALEX ANDREW
COUNCILOR L.C5 KAYABWE TOWN COUNCIL
DEPUTY SPEAKER MPIGI DISTRICT
DEPUTY HEAD TEACHER HOTS
